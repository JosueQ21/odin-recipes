Chicken Strips

Description

Crispy, juicy, and oh so very high in protein. Loved by all too!

  • Buffalo Wing Seasoning Mix
  • Boneless, skinless Chicken Tenders
  • Reduced-fat mayonnaise
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Buttery Spread
  • Blue Cheese Salad Dressing
  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Link a baking sheet with foil.
  2. Put seasoning mix in a plastic bag. Add chicken and shake to coat.
  3. Roll 1 to 2 pieces of chicken at a time with mayo to lightly coat.
  4. Roll each piece in bread crumbs.
  5. Place chicken on the prepared baking sheet and dot them with buttery spread.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear. About 15 minutes.
  7. Serve with dressing on the side.
